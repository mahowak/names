Scottish name data was taken from;

Crook, Alice Louise (2012) Personal naming patterns in Scotland, 1700 - 1800: a comparative study of the parishes of Beith, Dingwall, Earlston, and Govan. MPhil(R) thesis, University of Glasgow.

http://theses.gla.ac.uk/4190/

2012crookmphil.pdf

the records were preprocessed to standardise alternate spellings of the smae name (e.g. Ann / Anne / Hanna) and to remove obvious bynames that had been mistranscribed as prefix-names (e.g. females named McVey).
